◆ Our Story

Built for People Who Eat
With Intention

TruFork started with a simple frustration — every "Top 10 Udaipur Restaurants" list was the same five rooftops catering to tourists, not the city.

Our Mission

"To make the food knowledge of Udaipur's locals — the grandmothers, the rickshaw drivers, the neighbourhood sweetmakers — as discoverable as any five-star restaurant."

The Problem We Solve

When you search for food in Udaipur, you find the same 10 tourist-facing restaurants — polished menus, Instagram-ready tables, and prices that don't reflect local reality. What you don't find: the 7am kachori stall at Hathi Pol, the home kitchen that makes ker sangri the old way, or the chai wallah whose cardamom comes from his own garden.

TruFork is the antidote. Every dish and restaurant on our platform is community-verified and heritage-traced. Our authenticity scores are built from local testimony — not paid reviews, not SEO tricks.

What We Stand For
🏺
Heritage First
We trace dish origins, sourcing practices, and cooking methods. If it's been done the same way for 30+ years, we celebrate it.
👥
Community Powered
Our best content comes from locals — rickshaw drivers, professors, home cooks. Not influencers. Real people with real opinions.
🔍
Radical Honesty
We'll tell you when a popular dish at a famous spot isn't actually that good. Authenticity means telling the truth, even when it's unpopular.
🌿
Local Economy
Every recommendation we make is a vote for a local vendor. We exist to direct food tourism to those who deserve it most.
